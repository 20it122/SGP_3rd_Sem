We are one of those companys that envision the connection between the builders and the raw material suppliers.

There are some of the companys that don't get the best material or don't get the desired price for their raw material so we envision them to get what they desire and we also have the facility to connect builder to builder of govt to builder. We have a portal where the builder posts the tender and then the other builder can see and can propose their tender and as per need they can chose whom to give the tender and we also have a portal where the builder can have the direct connection with the raw material supplier and can directly contact them for the choice of their requirement.
